hodl

Light crypto wallet. TUI. Rust + ratatui.

What

  • Light wallet — talks to RPC, no full-node sync.
  • Multi-chain. Bitcoin and Ethereum first.
  • BIP-39 seed, BIP-32/44 derivation. Local-only key storage.
  • Cross-platform: Linux, macOS, Windows.

Status

Early scaffold. Not usable yet.
